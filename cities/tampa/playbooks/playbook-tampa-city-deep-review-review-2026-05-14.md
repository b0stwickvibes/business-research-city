## 1 — Market thesis

**Spend gravity (Channelside spine):** Premium celebration & waterfront **channel adjacency** — prioritize narratives tied to **Garrison Channel / Sparkman Wharf / cruise-port aquarium-arena walk-offs**, not undifferentiated “downtown Tampa” pins.

**Water Street (Strategic Properties overlay):** **Thousand & One / 1001 Water St** and district ground retail stack monetize **affluent residential + medical + hotel** compression. Treat as **`corporate_podium_policy = review`** when chasing **office shells** for nightclub adaptive reuse.

**Arena & stadium halos (calendar overlays — not walk-score proofs):**

| Venue                                              | Role                                                                                                                                          |
| -------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Amalie Arena**                                   | Lightning + concerts — **primary** spikes **walking** to Channelside retail & Sparkman Wharf.                                                 |
| **Raymond James Stadium**                          | Bucs — **northwest Tampa** (~8–12 mi straight-line from downtown core depending on pin)—model **rideshare compression**, not pedestrian halo. |
| **USF on-campus stadium (open ~Fall 2027 target)** | Collegiate football calendar — **schedule confidence medium** until NCAA release; treat as **growth overlay** not current revenue gate.       |

**Liquor receipts:** **No** Florida-wide public mixed-beverage ladder comparable to Texas—`anchors.json` receipts_notes apply.
