### About the Property

An exceptional opportunity to lease a turnkey, second-generation restaurant space with well-below market rent in the heart of Flagler Village—one of Fort Lauderdale’s most dynamic and rapidly evolving urban districts. This fully built-out space is licensed for full-service restaurant and bar use and is ideally suited for an operator looking to launch quickly with minimal upfront capital and no construction delays.
The existing tenant will deliver the premises in pristine, move-in ready condition, with all furniture, fixtures, and equipment remaining in place. This allows an incoming operator to seamlessly step into a proven, high-quality environment, accelerating time to revenue. The opportunity is offered with $250,000 in key money for the FF&E.
Situated within a 385-unit luxury apartment community with ground-floor retail, the space benefits from a built-in customer base and consistent foot traffic. The property is strategically located at the gateway to Flagler Village, directly across from the vibrant F.A.T. Village arts district and just three blocks from the Brightline station, providing seamless connectivity to Miami and West Palm Beach.
Join a strong and growing tenant mix including Salon Lofts, Mitch’s Bagels, BFT Fitness, Dog Drop, Go Grocer, and more, all contributing to the neighborhood’s energy and daily traffic.
This is a rare opportunity to secure a plug-and-play restaurant in a premier location with favorable lease economics—ideal for both established operators and emerging concepts looking to establish a presence in one of South Florida’s most sought-after submarkets.
This is a lease assignment. Tenant originally signed a 15-year lease plus three (3) additional 5-year options. Initial lease term expires in February 2038.
Tenant is still in business. Please do not disturb tenant or their employees. View Matterport Walkthrough: https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=sK9kUPMjuBG&
