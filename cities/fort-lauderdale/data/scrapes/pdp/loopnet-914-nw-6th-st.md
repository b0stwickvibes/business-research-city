### Property Overview

Position your business in the heart of one of Fort Lauderdale’s most rapidly evolving urban corridors at 914 NW 6th Street—a freestanding office/retail building situated just minutes from Flagler Village, Brightline Station, and Downtown Fort Lauderdale.
This property is surrounded by a dynamic mix of residential density, creative users, and emerging retail concepts. Immediate proximity to FAT Village and Thrive Arts and Wellness District further enhances its appeal, placing tenants at the center of the city’s cultural and entrepreneurial energy, alongside a wave of newly delivered mixed-use apartment communities driving consistent foot traffic and growth.
The building is currently configured for office use but offers a highly adaptable layout that can be easily converted into an open, modern environment. Whether for a wellness or fitness concept, charter school, creative office, retail showroom, or experiential use, the space lends itself to a wide range of applications. Tenants will also benefit from ample shared parking and the ability to activate exterior yard and patio areas.
Available for lease in its entirety or as smaller, demised suites, the property provides flexible configurations to accommodate a variety of users:
\*\*Suite 100: ±1,898 SF
\*\*Suite 103: ±2,260 SF (fronting NW 6th Street)
\*\*Suite 200: ±4,238 SF (second floor)
Opportunities exist to lease full floors or combine suites for a larger footprint, allowing tenants to scale within the building as needed.
914 NW 6th Street presents a rare opportunity to establish a presence in a high-growth pocket of Fort Lauderdale, where culture, connectivity, and community converge.
