## 1. Market thesis — “Booster / spectacle” intersection

Crossing **mixed-beverage economics** (where downloaded) with **UT football / Moody** calendars supports **two** viable **`ut-moody-core`** sub-corridors:

### A. West 6th / Warehouse District — wealth anchor

Mature-money **hotel + dining** gravity; strongest **year-round baseline** potential for receipt-heavy concepts. Look for **standalone / adaptive reuse**, **alley load-in**, **verticality** (rooftop second bar).

### B. Red River Cultural District — Moody funnel

Blocks **along Red River / Neches** with **walkable egress from Moody** (~**8th–10th** logic — tighten on maps). **Higher tolerance** for amplified / live-music land use than **Rainey residential canyon**. **510 Neches** geocodes to **6th & Neches / Red River** — **not** Rainey Street.

### Discarded / caution zones

| Zone                                 | Why                                                                                                                                                                                  |

| **Rainey Street / lakeshore towers** | Severe **high-rise residential** compression; new builds face **strict mitigation** — acoustic engineer before any lease.                                                            |
